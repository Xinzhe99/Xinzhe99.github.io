I am a PhD candidate at [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn), specializing in **Image Fusion** and **Microscopic Algorithms**. My research focuses on developing advanced computer vision techniques for multi-focus image fusion, leveraging deep learning and transformer architectures.

Research Interests
======
* Multi-focus Image Fusion
* Deep Learning (CNNs, Transformers, State Space Models)
* Microscopic Image Processing
* Computer Vision
